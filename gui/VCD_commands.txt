To get dump of simulation:

open_vcd NAME.vcd
log_vcd /TB FILE/dut/*
run all (make sure to restart sim)
close_vcd

find NAME.vcd in Vivado directory

open_vcd test.vcd
log_vcd /single_cycle_tb/dut/clk
